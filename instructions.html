Instructions for Employees to use TimePort



Acro TimePort website address is: http://www.acrocorp.com/timeport/

 

1) Open the website using an internet browser like Internet Explorer or Google Chrome. The login page looks like the page shown below.

2) Selection Employee button on the top.

3)  Enter the user id and password provided to you by your Employee Relations Specialist. If you do not have the login credentials, please contact your Employee Relations Specialist at Acro.

4)  After you have successfully logged in, you should see the time sheet entry page as shown below.

5)  Your name and client site are displayed on the screen.

6)  Select a weekending date from the dropdown list.

7)   Scan the timesheet approved by your manager and upload the scanned page (.PDF or .JPG formats only) using the Upload Time Sheet option on the screen.

8)  If you have an expense to report for the week, scan the expense page approved by your manager and upload the scanned page (.PDF or .JPG formats only) using the Upload Expense Sheet option on the screen.

9) Enter the straight time hours, over time (if any) and double time (if any) in the textboxes under Monday through Sunday. These hours must match the hours on the scanned sheet. You must use the entry format (either hour.minutes or decimals) displayed below the entry table. If you need to convert one format to another, please refer to the conversion table link available below the entry table. The system will automatically calculate the daily and weekly totals.

10) Enter your shift differentials or any other comments in the Comments box.

11)  Click the Submit button once done with the entries.

12) You can view your previous entries by clicking the View Time/Expense History link.

13) You can also change your password by using the Change Password button.

14) Make sure to log out of the system by using the Logout button.
